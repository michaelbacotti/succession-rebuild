About Succession Holding LLC

Succession Holding LLC is a family-owned holding company that owns residential and recreational real estate in Colorado, New Hampshire, Alaska, New Mexico, and New York. The properties include the Blackhawk Ranch cabin in Black Hawk, Colorado; the Ashuelot Cabin in southwestern New Hampshire; the Eagle River Home in Anchorage, Alaska; the Costilla Acres and Colorado City Lots land parcels in southern Colorado; the Hawthorne Acres parcel in central New York; and the Sweetwater Acres lot in Rio Rancho, New Mexico. Each property is held in a dedicated single-purpose LLC wholly owned by Succession Holding LLC, with all financial activity consolidated at the holding-company level for annual tax reporting.

What this site is for

The articles section is the editorial core of the site. Each article addresses a specific concept in real estate investing — cap rates, NOI, single-family versus multi-family investing, the 1031 exchange, mortgage financing, equity leveraging, and the seasonal and regional patterns that affect property values. The articles are written for an audience that is considering or has begun direct ownership of investment real estate and wants to deepen their understanding of how the underlying mechanics work. The articles are not investment advice; they are educational material grounded in the specific markets where the holding company has direct experience.

Editorial standards

Every article is reviewed before publication. Numerical claims are tied to named sources: Bureau of Labor Statistics for inflation data, U.S. Census Bureau for housing inventory data, Federal Reserve Economic Data for mortgage rate trends, county assessor offices for property valuation data, and state-specific landlord-tenant statutes for legal references. Where jurisdictional specifics matter (eviction procedures, security deposit limits, rent control statutes), the relevant state and statute are named inline. The articles are not promotional; they do not advocate for or against any specific investment approach, and they explicitly note when a concept applies differently in different markets.

The market analysis section tracks trends in the specific markets where the holding company operates. Each analysis piece cites the data source for the trend being discussed. The education section covers concepts that come up repeatedly in real estate investing but are not specific to any single transaction or property — concepts like leverage, taxation of rental income, depreciation, and the distinction between cash flow and total return.

What this site is not

This site is not a real estate brokerage. It does not list properties for sale. It does not accept advertising, sponsored content, or paid placements. It does not provide personalized investment advice. The articles are educational material; readers should consult a licensed real estate professional, tax adviser, and attorney before making any specific real estate investment decision. The holding company does not solicit outside investors.

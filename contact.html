We are not a brokerage, a funding platform, or a property management company. We do not earn commissions on deals, receive referral fees from service providers, or accept payment to feature specific investments or companies. This structure allows us to write about what we think matters, rather than what generates the most revenue.

What Makes Succession Holding Different

Real estate content online runs the spectrum from genuinely useful to dangerously misleading. At one end, you have sites that exist to collect leads and resell them to agents or investors. At the other, you have publications that take time to explain how things actually work — the mechanics, the math, the trade-offs — without promising quick riches or pushing a single strategy as right for everyone. We try to be the latter.

Every article we publish is written to the standard of general-interest financial publications: well-researched, carefully edited, and organized around concepts that hold up over time rather than short-term market speculation. We reference primary sources where appropriate, and we clearly distinguish between educational content and opinion. When we update an article because market conditions have changed, we note that in the article header.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept guest posts or contributed articles?
Currently, we do not accept unsolicited contributed content. All articles on Succession Holding are produced by our editorial team or commissioned writers who follow our editorial guidelines. If you have a topic suggestion, send it over — we read every inquiry.

Do you have a affiliate relationships with any products or services you recommend?
No. We do not use affiliate links. When we mention a book, tool, or service, it is because we think it is genuinely useful — not because we earn anything from the recommendation.

How often do you update your articles?
Articles covering current market conditions (mortgage rates, housing supply, regulatory changes) are reviewed when relevant data changes. Articles covering foundational concepts (how cap rates work, how to read a rent roll) are reviewed annually. All articles show their original publication date; significant updates are noted in the header.

Can I reprint or republish your content?
Our content is copyright Succession Holding LLC. We do not allow reproduction on other sites without written permission. If you are an educator or journalist who wants to reference our work, reach out and we will typically grant permission with appropriate attribution.

Why does this site show ads?
Display advertising is our primary revenue source. It allows us to operate an independent editorial publication without charging subscription fees or accepting sponsored content. All ads are clearly labeled and served through Google AdSense. Our editorial team does not review or approve specific advertisements, and ad content does not influence what we write about or how we cover any topic.

Response Time and Expectations

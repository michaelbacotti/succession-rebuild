Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They are used to remember your preferences, authenticate users, and analyze site traffic. Most web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, blocking cookies may affect some site functionality.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted).
